By utilizing techniques like fluid layouts, media queries, and responsive images, I ensure that the website's content and design elements adjust dynamically to provide optimal viewing experiences. Touch-friendly interactions and cross-browser compatibility are also prioritized, enabling users to navigate and engage with the site smoothly across various devices and web browsers.
